About Me

Hi, I’m Johnny Faris – a dedicated husband, proud father of a spirited two-year-old daughter, and a passionate personal trainer with a unique background in professional MMA. My journey in the world of fitness and martial arts has taken me across the globe, with intensive training experiences in Thailand and Brazil. These experiences have enriched my approach to personal training, allowing me to bring diverse, effective techniques to my clients. More

Since 2015, I’ve been committed to helping individuals achieve their fitness goals. My career as a personal trainer began at Equinox, where I honed my skills over five years, working with a wide range of clients. I hold various well-accredited continuing education certifications in fitness and health, which keep me updated with the latest and most effective training methods.

One of my specialties is weight management, particularly through the rigorous and disciplined practice of weight cutting that I mastered during my MMA career. I understand the complexities and challenges of body transformation, and I apply this knowledge to help clients achieve their desired weight safely and effectively.

Injury prevention and corrective exercises are another cornerstone of my training philosophy. I have extensive experience designing programs that not only enhance performance but also safeguard against injuries. This is especially crucial for my work with professional athletes, where I focus on building explosive strength and optimizing athletic performance.

I’m passionate about functional movement and believe in building muscle the right way. My approach is holistic, ensuring that every client not only looks good but feels strong, agile, and resilient. Whether you’re aiming to improve your athletic performance, recover from an injury, or simply get in the best shape of your life, I’m here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Join me on a fitness journey that’s tailored to your needs and grounded in expertise. Let’s unlock your potential together!
